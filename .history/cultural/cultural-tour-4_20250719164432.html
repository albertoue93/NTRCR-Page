Guanacaste Rum & Brewery Experience Tour.

  • Filadelfia, Guanacaste, Costa Rica.

This tour is located approximately 50 minutes from the Liberia International Airport.

It is a tour where a little of our culture in the province of Guanacaste and the history of our ancestors is highlighted (“Sabaneros”, typical food, instruments such as the Marimba and ancient utensils).

Our first activity will be carried out in one of the most recognized sugar cane companies in our province. Here you can experience everything from cultivation to the art of rum distillation and the craft beer process.

After the rum and beer activities, we will enjoy a delicious typical lunch cooked with firewood and handmade tortillas , as is the tradition.

We will finish with a short tour of one of the archaeological cantons of the province of Guanacaste, where brief explanation will be given about our “ Chorotega Tribe ” ancestors and the entire canton.

We returned to our place of stay.

Included

  • Private Transport.
  • Bilingual Tour Guide.
  • Activities.
  • Typical lunch.
  • Bottles of purified water.
  • Optional: Souvenir stop.

Recomendations (What To Bring)

  • Comfortable Cool Clothes.
  • Comfortable Shoes.
  • Sunscreen.
  • Mosquito Repellent.
  • Sun glasses.
  • Poncho in case it rains.
