Coffee Tour Guanacaste.

$175/

per person
  • 4 Days / 5 Night
    • 4 Days / 5 Night
    • Max People : 40
    • Hojancha, Guanacaste, Costa Rica.

    Diriá Coffee Tour is located in Hojancha, Guanacaste, Costa Rica. 89 kilometers (55.2 miles) from Daniel Oduber Quiròs Airport (LIR). This coffee processor is characterized by being the only coffee producers in one of the five blue zones of the world.

    We also know that you have heard that Costa Rica produces very high quality coffees. Learn from the experts how to produce, select micro lots, gourmet coffees and specialty coffees. For 1:30 minutes, the experts will guide you through all the processes from the germination of the plant to the process of roasting and cupping the coffee. Discover in this site one of the secrets of longevity in the blue zone.

    After the tour, a coffee tasting with snacks will be taken. We will finish and head to Rancho Doña Elena Restaurant to enjoy a delicious Traditional (Casado) lunch.

    Then we will go and a small demonstration will be held in the Nicoya Guanacaste park and we will see the church of San Blas de Nicoya, almost four centuries old, declared of historical-archaeological interest. Here the tour ends and we will return to the starting point. It depends on the client's place of stay, if you prefer, we will make a small visit to the Santa Cruz Park or Filadelfia to see its beautiful archeology that stands out in these Cantons.
