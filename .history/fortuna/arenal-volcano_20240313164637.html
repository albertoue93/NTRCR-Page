Arenal Volcano Tours.

It is located 86 kilometers (53.4 miles) northeast of the Daniel Oduber Quirós International Airport (LIR) and 78 kilometers (48.4 miles) northeast of the City of Liberia Guanacaste. In the Province of Alajuela.

This small town is located in a valley between the Miravalles Volcano and the Tenorio Volcano, the perfect place to enjoy its beautiful nature.

It is a jungle in this part of the Province of Alajuela near the border between the province of Guanacaste and because it is a biological corridor it is home to many tropical species such as mammals, amphibians, reptiles and birds.

National Park Hike And Hot Springs

  • San Carlos, Alajuela, Costa Rica.

This option takes you on a visit, take a tour of the Arenal Volcano National Park, through its trails, you choose the one you want.

  • Heliconias Trail: (1 kilometer) the path is flat with a narrow section and is covered with vegetation and you can see the crater of the volcano.
  • Las Coladas Trail: (1.7 kilometers) in this you can see the volcanic cone, Lake Arenal, and La Sierra de Tilarán.
  • Ceibo Trail: (2.3 kilometers) circular route, we can see a large Ceiba Petranda that is more than 400 years old.

We will move from this place to have lunch at La Choza del Laurel (typical food) and to finish we will go to a relaxing hot springs place (Baldi Hot Springs Resort) for 4 hours maximum.

Finally, after having enjoyed this place, we will return to your place of stay.

Package 01 National Park Hike And Hot Springs

  • San Carlos, Alajuela, Costa Rica.

This option has a walk of approximately two hours and thirty minutes within the Tenorio Volcano National Park. Here we will find natural magic in a unique landscape.

The color of the "Río Celeste" popularly called (Buena Vista its original name), surrounded by a lush forest, offers a site worth seeing and admiring. It is a river with crystal clear waters that turn turquoise. It is a natural spectacle that can only be witnessed at the Teñideros site. It is a magical place that offers a wide variety of possibilities to observe the wonders of nature and phenomena that are difficult to find. Also right here you can see where the crystal clear waters (Río Quebrada Agria) and the Color Celeste river (Río Buena Vista) meet.

Then we will have lunch at "El Sabor de Doña Carmen", a very traditional place with a beautiful butterfly garden. Next we will travel for 15 minutes and take a walk of one hour and thirty minutes along the trails of Spring Paradise Bijagua, where we will find sightings of different types of plant species, orchids, birds, amphibians, reptiles and mammals such as: sloths, with luck we will be able to see tapirs, monkeys and more...

Finally we will return to the starting place, but on the way we will detour for approximately an hour, to also reach a beautiful waterfall called Llanos del Cortés, where you can take a natural bath with a duration of about 40 minutes of time there in that place.

Optional: Souvenir Stop.

Package 02 Sky Adventures

  • Santa Teresa, Upala, Costa Rica

This option has a 2-hour tour in (Tree Chocolate) through the chocolate plantations, its process from the seed to obtaining its result. Also the process of planting and tasting coconut and other plants.

In this place we will be able to see and hopefully enjoy the most common toucans in the area (Collared Aracari, Yellow - Throated Toucan and Keel billed Toucan) and other species of birds. Right there you can get products made from Theobroma cacao (chocolate).

Then we head to route #1 (North Interamerican) where we will take a small detour to visit a beautiful waterfall called Llanos del Cortés. In that place you can enjoy 1 hour of a natural bath if you wish.

Finally, we will return to the starting point.

Optional: Souvenir Stop (It depends on where the clients have their staying).

Package 03 Hike to Arenal Volcano National Park and La Fortuna Waterfall

  • Tenorio Volcano National Park, Costa Rica.

In this option we will take a tour of approximately 2.30 minutes within the Tenorio Volcano National Park.

In this place you can admire and enjoy the Natural Magic that exists in its "Río Celeste" popularly called, its waters are tinted with a turquoise color that makes it unique.

We continue the course where we will reach a waterfall, then a viewpoint where you can see the vegetation of the forest and lastly where the river of light blue waters (Buena Vista) with the river of crystal clear waters (Quebrada Agria) join together to give a beautiful natural spectacle.

Immediately after leaving the park we will go to have lunch at Soda Doña Carmen where you can enjoy a traditional meal where we can find a beautiful butterfly garden and the great possibility of bird watching in the area.

Then we will move to Bijagua Ranas, a place where we can take a tour of approximately one hour along the trails where we will find sloths, birds (toucans, mommots and more), there the environment of many species of spectacular frogs such as: red-eyed frog (Agalychnis callidryas). Blue Jeans (Oophaga pumilio) and appreciating the habitat of each species.

Finally we will move and go to a beautiful waterfall called Llanos del Cortés, where you can take a natural bath for 30 minutes.

We will leave that place and go back to the place of departure (your accommodation)

Optional: Souvenir Stop.

Package 04 Hanging Bridges And Hot Springs

  • Santa Teresa, Upala, Costa Rica.

It depends on where you stay until the rainforest. Our first activity is the National Park, a walk of approximately two hours and thirty minutes within it, seeing its beautiful waterfall "Río Celeste", its popular name.

Then we will go to the dyeing mills. On this journey we will be able to appreciate a beautiful viewpoint, in addition to a turquoise lagoon and the union of a river with crystal clear waters (Quebrada Agria) and the beautiful turquoise river originally called (Buena Vista).

After appreciating its natural landscape in this park, we will move to the center of the "Bijagua" district to have lunch and then in that same place we will take a walk on some trails in search of fauna, the highlight being sloths, toucans, other birds and more fauna.

We will carry out the last activity, closing with a more cultural touch, a tour of Coffee, chocolate and sugar cane. This is how we will end part of the afternoon, returning to our starting point.

Optional: Souvenir stop (here depends on time and traffic).
