Cancelation Policy

  • 1. All cancellations are subject to a 10% service charge.
  • 2. To make any change to the date of your service you must notify 72 hours in advance, send an email (newtravelsreasonscostarica@gmail.com) or WhatsApp (+506 89324763) and receive our response and affirmation. Since this will be a support for both our clients and our company.
  • 3, Any service offered by our company, the client must pay a 50% deposit to reserve the service they choose. You can pay the other 50% of the reserved service with any of these two options:
  • In the event that the client cannot attend the tour due to force majeure due to any last-minute situation that arises, such as: fractures in some part of the body, chronic illnesses out of control, childbirth, complications in the pregnancy, traumatic accident, ophidic accident, death and more, which definitely gets out of control; Our company will return 50% of the reservation made by the client. With a maximum of fifteen days to return the money.
  • Cancellations before 72 hours, deposits will be refunded.
  • Cancellations with less than 72 hours, the company will retain 100% of the deposit.
  • Deposits for private catamaran tours are non-refundable if cancellation is made less than 30 days prior to the booked day.
  • If our clients wish to change the date of the reservation, they must notify the company 72 hours before departure and send an email (newtravelsreasonscostarica@gmail.com or whats app +(506)89324763) with the request, since this It will be of support, both for the client who makes the reservation and for our company.
  • The customer must be at the pickup location five minutes before the indicated pickup time.
  • Note: Regarding the points (2,3, 5) mentioned above in the cancellation policies, you must proceed in this way because the company is responsible for the payment of the reservations for the operations (transport, guide and tourist sites).
