We provide the best tour facilities.

We are a local company located in the province of Guanacaste, Carrillo, Sardinal. We invite all our visitors to get to know the natural paradise and the great biodiversity that our country has. As well as the culture of our ancestors. Guanacaste, we are the second largest province in Costa Rica, one of the most notable for having beautiful beaches, a spectacular volcanic mountain range, Costa Rican "Folklor" for its customs, it also has the Golfo de Nicoya which is in fifth place in the world for having "Blue Zones" healthy, longer-lived people and its people "Pura Vida!!.

Company Overview

New Travels Reasons Costa Rica was founded on January 20, 2022. Through a dream come true, hard work, family support, and, above all, a lot of love and passion as a local guide for the Tempisque Conservation Area and a naturalist, which characterize me.

Our main objective as a company is to provide our clients with high-quality private services adapting to their preferences. in the Province of Guanacaste and the entire country.

We specialize in providing our clients with safe transportation from Costa Rica's International Airports, hotels, and private accommodations such as condos, Airbnb, and more.

Private tours are available throughout the province of Guanacaste, Fortuna de San Carlos, the Bijagua Rainforest in the province of Alajuela, the Monteverde Cloud Forest in the province of Puntarenas, and the entire country if our clients so desire.

We work in conjunction with local accommodation owners in the Guanacaste area and other areas according to our clients' preferences.

Our Mission:

  • Is to provide unparalleled experiences to our tourists.
  • We focus on our clients' unique needs to create exceptional experiences.
  • We guarantee punctual and reliable services for transfers and excursions.
  • We maintain rigorous safety standards with periodic fleet inspections and communication during services with highly trained guides or drivers certified by our country's government.
  • We offer a relaxing travel experience with vehicles in perfect condition, professional drivers, and experienced guides certified by the Costa Rican Tourism Institute (ICT).
  • The safety and needs of our clients will always be our priority.
